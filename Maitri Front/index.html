About and Vision

Buddha Institute of Technology, Gorakhpur also known as BIT, Gorakhpur, established in 2009, is a private degree engineering college situated in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. Buddha Institute of Technology (BIT) is one of the top technical colleges in Gorakhpur. The institute comes under the aegis of Buddha Group of Institutions. BIT offers undergraduate, postgraduate and diploma programs in the discipline of Engineering and Polytechnic.

To be recognized as leader in the region providing technical education and research by developinf professionals with required attributes to take up socio-economic challenges through in line with pedagogical changes and socio economic requirements.
